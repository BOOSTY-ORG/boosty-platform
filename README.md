# boosty-platform
Developing of an admin dashboard and CRM for Boosty, a solar energy company targeting Nigeria and West Africa.
The system will allow users to apply for solar energy systems funded by investors, with repayments based on creditworthiness. The platform will be AI-powered, calculating costs based on household appliances.

** Boosty is an energy company focused on providing renewable energy solutions, starting with Nigeria and aiming to expand to West Africa and eventually the entire continent. **
** The concept involves a pool system where investors fund others to get renewable energy, with users like Ayomide applying for solar systems and paying back based on creditworthiness. **
** The platform is AI-powered, allowing users to interact and calculate costs based on their household appliances. **
** The dashboard will be used by Boosty staff to manage investors, solve user problems, and handle payments. **
